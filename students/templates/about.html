About Gurukul Classes Biraul

Our Institute

Gurukul Classes Biraul is a well-known coaching institute located in Biraul, Darbhanga (Bihar). We provide quality education for students from Class 8 to 12 with special focus on Science stream, board exams, and competitive exams.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide strong conceptual education, disciplined learning environment, and personal attention to every student so they can achieve success in board and entrance exams.
